03

questions worth answering.

what happens when i hit my monthly limit?
your key returns 429 monthly limit exceeded with the remaining quota in the body. no surprise overage bills — you opt into the next tier when you're ready, or wait for the calendar month to reset.
how fresh is the data?
every /v1/price-check call hits live listings from the underlying marketplaces with a 5-minute cache layer in front. for slow-moving categories we also blend in stored observations from recent searches so you get a stable median even when listings churn.
do you handle marketplace tos / scraping risk?
yes — that's the value. naya operates the scrapers, rotates infrastructure, handles blocks, and absorbs platform changes. you get a stable { medianPrice, p25, p75, dealScore } contract.
can i pay annually?
growth and scale tiers can be billed annually with a 15% discount. email hello@naya.so with your billing details.
what categories beyond fashion?
today: vintage clothing, sneakers, accessories, and select campus-localized inventory. on the roadmap: home goods and collectibles. tell us what you'd buy and we'll prioritize.
