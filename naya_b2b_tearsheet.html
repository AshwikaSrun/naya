resale price intelligence, as a single api call.

naya gives shopping apps, browser extensions, and inventory engines a live, normalized view of secondhand prices across ebay, grailed, depop, and poshmark. one bearer token, three endpoints, no scrapers to maintain.

01
what

price-check + cross-listings

median, p25, p75, and a deal score for any query — plus the same item, cheaper, on a different platform.

02
how

one bearer, json, sub-3s.

bring your own client. naya runs the scrapers, deduplicates listings, rotates infrastructure, and absorbs platform changes for you.

03
why

ship in an afternoon.

zero scraping risk on your side. transparent monthly limits, no per-call overages, kill-switch revocable keys.

endpoints

v1 · railway-hosted
